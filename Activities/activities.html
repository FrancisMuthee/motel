Activities

White water rafting down the Sagana river is one of our guest favourites. During this trip with our certified guides, you are able to plunge, experience natural slides, (depending on the season) enter caves and experience level 4 rapids

Nature Walk

Explore the rich valleys of Murang`a county where the
Mau Mau settled. Our nature walks vary from the river
trails, irrigation trails, and canyon trails. The various
sceneries allow you to explore the unique bird, animal
and tree species.
